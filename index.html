About

I am a PhD student in the Analysis Group at the University of St Andrews, supervised by Jonathan Fraser and Kenneth Falconer. Before this I obtained my MMath in Pure Mathematics from the University of St Andrews in 2024.

My research interests lie in fractal geometry, dimension theory and dynamical systems. Recently I have mainly been working with Assouad-type dimensions.
